The CyberPony Project

The origin Story

The Horseriding sport did not change their training practices for ages. The technology was never developped to improve training of both riders and horses, as it is the case is almost any other sport. Over the last few years a handfull of companies and startup are trying to bridge this gap but the knowledge and investment is lacking and most of the projects results in expensive marketing stunts that provide few or no help over the long run. Nonetheless the need is present to help lower the barrier of entry by reducung the training time to reach a high level, and make it appealing to the new generations. By launching this Open Source project i hope to leverage the talent from this awesome community to be able to create meaningfull products that directly answer a need and being able te make is financially accessible to anybody.
